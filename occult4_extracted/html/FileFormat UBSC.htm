    0 = star not know to be perturbed;
    1 = HIP Mult_flag C, resolved double or multiple star;
    2 = HIP Mult_flag X, stocahstic (or failed) solution;
    3 = HIP Mult_flag G, accelerating star;
    4 = HIP Mult_flag V, variability-induced motion;
    5 = HIP Mult_flag O, physical binary with an orbital solution;
    6 = an astrometric binary, candidate unresolved binary with statistically

	and not in categories 1-5;
    7 = an astrometric binary, from Makarov & Kaplan 2005 [2005AJ....129.2420M]
        catalog.
