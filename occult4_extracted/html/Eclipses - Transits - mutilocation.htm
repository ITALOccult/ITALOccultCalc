Transits - MultiLocation predictions

Contact time transit predictions for multiple locations are generated from the Transit prediction form, by selecting a relevant Site file.

Explanation

ColumnComments
SiteThe name of the site location
Longitude, LatitudeThe site coordinates, rounded to the nearest minutes ending in 5.
ElevnThe altitude of the site
Exterior IngressThe UT, PA, and altitude of the Sun, at the time of exterior ingress.
Interior IngressThe UT and PA at the time of interior ingress.
MaximumThe UT, PA of the planet relative to the center of the sun, and the altitude of the Sun, at the time of maximum transit.
Interior EgressThe UT and PA at the time of interior egress.
Exterior EgressThe UT, PA, and altitude of the Sun, at the time of exterior egress.
Min SepThe distance (arcsec) between the centers of the sun and planet at maximum transit.

Important When the event occurs on the day preceding the date of the prediction, a '-' or '+' follows the UT time. In the example. Exterior ingress occurs at 22hrs UT on June 5.

Display controls

The prediction can be sorted by any of the Site name, Longitude, or Latitude - using the menu item Sort....

You can control whether a blank line is included after every 5th line with the menu item with Prediction... Include 5th line break
