Object altitude

From this form you can determine the altitude of an object or its time of rise and set.

Specify the objects J2000 RA and declination. The routine will precess that position to the date specified.

Specify your location. The default values when the form is opened are the values for your site set in the user settings.

Specify the date and time. If altitude is being computed, you need to specify the hour and minute. If rise/set times are desired, only the year+month+day is required - the hours and minutes are ignored.

Click Altitude - and the altitude of the object at the relevant time is displayed. The altitude is corrected for refraction.

Click Rise/Set times, and the Local times of Rise and set are displayed - with the conversion from UT to local time being based on the Time Zone specified on the form. If the object never rises, or never sets (is circumpolar), a message to the effect is given.
