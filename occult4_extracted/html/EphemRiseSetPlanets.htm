Rise and Set times of the Planets

The times of rise and set of the major planets are computed for the site latitude and longitude, and are corrected for the Time Zone of the site. The predictions cover the Sun, and all planets to Neptune. The following is an example of the prediction.

Times are given on a 24hr clock. The times of rise and set of the planets are computed for the months specified, and at an interval of days as specified, at the top of the form. To allow for the effects of refraction and the diameter of the Sun, the times of rise and set are computed for when the geocentric position of the body has the following altitude:

  • Sun -0.83°
  • Planets -0.57°
