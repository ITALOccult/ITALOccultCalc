Eclipse and Transit Predictions - Overview

Solar Eclipse predictions

Generates predictions of Solar Eclipses. Predictions are grouped in a 10-year period that is specified by the user. Predictions include:

  • World map, showing eclipse path
  • Calculation of path coordinates, and local circumstances
  • Detailed maps, with right-click functionality to generate local circumstances
  • Drawing the eclipse curves on Google Earth
  • Generating a list of local circumstances for multiple sites
Predictions are fully valid for historical epochs to at least 2000 BC.

NOTE. The function for analysing Baily Bead timings includes an animation capability. This can be used to predict the occurrence and appearance of Baily's Beads for specified sites.

Lunar Eclipse predictions

Generates predictions of Lunar Eclipses. Predictions are grouped in a 10-year period that is specified by the user. Predictions include:

  • Contact times
  • Graphic showing the path of the moon through the umbra and penumbra
  • Graphic showing the hemisphere of the Earth where the moon is visible at each contact time.
Predictions are fully valid for historical epochs to at least 2000 BC.

Transits of Mercury and Venus

Generates predictions of transits. Predictions are grouped in a century period that is specified by the user. Predictions include:

  • Geocentric contact times
  • Graphic showing the path of the planet against the solar disk
  • If the transit is 'grazing', the coordinates of the line separating full transit from partial transit, or partial transit from no transit. [Note - no more than one of these lines can exist at any one transit.]
Predictions are fully valid for historical epochs.


Analysis of Baily's Beads

The provides a tool for the identification and analysis of Baily's Beads observed during an eclipse. The basic features include:

  • A single file for each observer, held in a subdirectory for that eclipse
  • Control over a range of plot parameters and ephemeris corrections
  • Ability to control which of the forms 'stay on top'
  • Control over the plotting of the graphic of the solar image, and the lunar limb
  • Ability to plot the results of grazing and ordinary occultations - to better define the lunar limb

Converting 'old' data files of Baily Bead observations

The button Convert old files is used to convert the '.ini' files of observations maintained under winOccult 3.x to the format used in this version of Occult. Note that if a file has been converted, then amended - the conversion button will not overwrite the amended data with the old data.
