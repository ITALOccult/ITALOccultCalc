Rise and Set times of the Moon

The times of rise and set of the Moon are computed for the site latitude and longitude, and are corrected for the Time Zone of the site.

The time of Rise and Set of the moon is calculated for the whole year, with the times of rise being formatted in a single table, as are the times of set. The times are computed for when the geocentric position of the moon is at an altitude of +0.10° [This corrects for the effects of refraction, the apparent diameter of the moon, and horizontal parallax, such that the upper limb of the moon is on the horizon at this time.]
