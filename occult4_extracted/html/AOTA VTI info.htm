Information on Video Time Inserters

The following are links to the home pages of a range of Video Time Inserters.

IMPORTANT. An issue for all GPS timing devices is to ensure that the device has a current 'GPS Almanac' to provide the current correction from GPS time to UTC time. The devices handle this issue in different ways. Some rely on batteries to maintain the almanac in memory for a long period of time (many weeks), while others retain the almanac for only a few days.. In use the almanac is regularly downloaded - and this should occur within the first 10 minutes of the GPS unit starting.
