Crater.dat, Crater.bin

CRATER.DAT contains co-ordinates of craters, and their diameter in km. The file is used to plot craters on a simple map of the moon. The data is in the form:
    aabbccNNNNNNNNNNNNNNNN
where
  • aa is the longitude (0.1 deg)
  • bb is the latitude (0.1 deg)
  • cc is the diameter (km)
  • NNNNNNNNNNNNNNNN is the name of the crater.
all held as 2-byte signed integer.

    aabbccNNNNNNNNNNNNNNNNNNNNT
where

  • bb is the latitude (0.1 deg)
  • cc is the diameter (0.1 km)
