Fresnel diffraction light curves

The following plots give predicted Fresnel-diffraction light curves for differing asteroid diameters and geocentric distances. They are based on the asteroid having a circular profile. For use in assessing whether the light drop from a small body is consistent with the source light.

The plots were prepared by Bob Anderson.
