Geodetic datums

The latitude and longitudes of sites are necessarily referred to a reference system - the Geodetic datum. Historically, geodetic datums have been defined for various regions around the world, and there are small differences between them. Occult converts the coordinates to the WGS84 system using the standard Molodenski equations - which involve functions of the difference in radius and ellipticity of the reference spheroid, and the offset of the centers of those ellipsoids (dX, dY, dZ).

OCCULT uses the following datums, together with the geocentric datum offsets (in metres). The list also includes the definitions of the datum codes used for the observations collected by RGO - with the conversion algorithm given below.
