UserStar_MinorPlanetSearch.csv

This file holds the details of a single star, for use in searching for occultations by asteroids or planets.

The file consists of a single line, with the data being held in comma-separated fields. The field definitions are:

FieldContent
0 Star name
1 Epoch of the star's position (eg 2015.5)
2 R.A. Hours
3 R.A. Mins
4 R.A. Secs
5 Annual proper motion in RA, in secs of time
6 Sign of declination
6 Dec. Degrees (including the sign of declination)
7 Dec. Mins
8 Dec. arcsecs
9 Annual proper motion in Dec, in arcsecs
10 Parallax, in arcsecs
11 Radial Velocity (km/sec)
12 Magnitude V
13 Magnitude R
14 Magnitude B
15 RUWE  [-1 if none available, 0 = not set in Gaia. Otherwise Gaia value.]
16 Duplicate Source flag [0 = false, 1 = true]
